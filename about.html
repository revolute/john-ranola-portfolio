Product and Service Designer

Hey there, I’m a multidisciplinary designer born and raised in Sydney, Australia. My fields of design expertise are in product, UX, and service design.

I believe creating great experiences needs a few things: a drive to build what’s right for the people that participate in these experiences, influence and rapport with key decision makers and good collaboration with the cross-disciplinary teams that build them.

Prior to working in design, I was a front-end engineer using technologies such as Less, Sass and Angular.

When I’m not designin’, you’ll most likely catch me hiking, camping, playing volleyball or chasing after my cat overlord.
