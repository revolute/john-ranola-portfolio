Before joining CommBank’s Digital Experience team I was a software engineer in our digital scrum teams, specialising in crafting pixel-perfect UI in an elegant way.

This knowledge has served me well through my various projects as a designer. Where engineering says a certain design decision is not worth the effort, I’m able to dig deep into the detail and suggest alternative ways to tackle the problem.

This usually results in getting an outcome that’s great for the user and doesn’t want to make engineers cry tears of pain. Everyone wins!
